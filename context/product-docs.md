# Product Knowledge Base — Nexora Workspace

> Used by the Customer Success Digital FTE to answer common customer questions.

---

## Account & Login

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
Go to nexora.io/login → click "Forgot password" → enter your email → check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires in 30 minutes.

**Q: How do I add a new team member?**
Go to Settings → Team Members → Invite User. Enter their email and choose their role (Viewer, Editor, or Admin). They'll receive an email invitation.

**Q: I'm locked out of my account.**
After 5 failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes. If you still can't log in after that, contact support — we can manually unlock it.

**Q: How do I enable Single Sign-On (SSO)?**
SSO is available on Business and Enterprise plans only. Go to Settings → Security → SSO and follow the SAML 2.0 setup guide. For Enterprise setup, contact your account manager.

## Billing & Payments

**Q: Where can I find my invoice?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Invoices. You can download PDF invoices for all past payments.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Method → Update Card. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

**Q: When is my next billing date?**
Your billing date is the same day of the month you originally subscribed. You can see the exact date in Settings → Billing → Overview.

**Q: Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Billing → Plan → Switch to Annual. You'll receive a prorated credit for unused days on your current plan.

**Q: I was charged twice this month.**
This occasionally happens when a payment method update coincides with a billing cycle. Contact support with your account email and we will investigate and refund the duplicate charge within 3–5 business days.

## Plans & Upgrades

**Q: How do I upgrade my plan?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Plan → Upgrade. Changes are immediate and you'll be charged a prorated amount.

**Q: What happens when I hit my user limit?**
You'll receive an email warning when you're at 80% capacity. At 100%, new invitations will be blocked until you upgrade.

**Q: Can I downgrade my plan?**
Yes, but only at the end of your current billing cycle. Go to Settings → Billing → Plan → Downgrade. Note: if you're over the user or storage limit for the lower plan, you'll need to reduce usage first.

## Integrations

**Q: How do I connect Slack?**
Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack → Connect. You'll be prompted to authorize the Nexora Slack app in your workspace.

**Q: The Google Workspace integration isn't syncing.**
Try disconnecting and reconnecting the integration under Settings → Integrations → Google Workspace. If the issue persists, check that your Google Admin has granted the necessary permissions.

**Q: Does Nexora integrate with Salesforce?**
Yes, Salesforce integration is available on Business and Enterprise plans. See our integration guide at nexora.io/docs/integrations/salesforce.

## Data & Security

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
All data is stored on AWS infrastructure in the US-East region. Enterprise customers can request EU or APAC region hosting.

**Q: Is Nexora SOC 2 compliant?**
Yes. Nexora is SOC 2 Type II certified. Our compliance report is available upon request for Enterprise customers.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
Go to Settings → Data → Export. You can export all projects, tasks, and documents as a ZIP file. Export requests are processed within 24 hours.

---

## Cancellations

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Billing → Plan → Cancel Subscription. Your account stays active until the end of the current billing period. You can reactivate at any time.

**Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel?**
We offer refunds within 14 days of the initial purchase or renewal for annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable. See our refund policy for full details.
