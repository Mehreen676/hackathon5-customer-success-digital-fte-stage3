## What Nexora Does

Nexora provides a cloud-based project management and team collaboration platform for mid-market companies (50–500 employees). The platform helps teams track projects, assign tasks, manage documents, and report on progress — all in one place.

**Core product:** Nexora Workspace
**Key features:**
- Task and project management
- Document storage and collaboration
- Team dashboards and reporting
- Integrations: Slack, Google Workspace, Jira, Salesforce
- Single Sign-On (SSO) support
